Hayao Miyazaki

Hayao Miyazaki (born January 5, 1941) is a renowned Japanese film director, producer, screenwriter, animator, author, and manga artist. He is best known for co-founding Studio Ghibli, one of the most successful and critically acclaimed animation studios in the world. Miyazaki's films are celebrated for their imaginative storytelling, richly detailed animation, and strong environmental and pacifist themes.

Work

  • My Neighbor Totoro (1988)
  • Spirited Away (2001)
  • Howl's Moving Castle (2004)
